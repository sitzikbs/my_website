3D Paintbrush: Local Stylization of 3D Shapes with Cascaded Score Distillation

2024-07-11 By Itzik Ben-Shabat

We are excited to introduce 3D Paintbrush, a novel method for local stylization of 3D shapes using cascaded score distillation. This research explores innovative approaches to applying artistic styles to 3D geometry while maintaining structural integrity.

Key Contributions

Our method enables artists and researchers to selectively apply different styles to specific regions of 3D objects, opening up new possibilities for creative expression in 3D content creation.

Technical Approach

The cascaded score distillation framework allows for fine-grained control over the stylization process, balancing between artistic expression and geometric fidelity.

For more details, please refer to our paper and code repository.
