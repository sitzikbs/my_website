Completed Stanford’s Machine Learning Course on Coursera

2017-01-30 By Itzik Ben-Shabat

Just finished my first online course on Machine Learning ! ! !

 

If you are new to this topic or find it interesting I totally recommend it.

The awesome Andrew Ng teaches the basics in a very interesting way, giving tons of application examples and not spending too much time on the mathematical proofs.

Forgot to mention that it’s absolutely FREE!  So why not give it a shot?

Here is the link:

https://www.coursera.org/learn/machine-learning

Now I’m going to try and learn something about these Deep Neural Networks that everyone is buzzing about…
