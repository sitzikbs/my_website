Triceratops process

note: This is not a step by step tutorial but rather a general documentation.

The jumpsuit was inspired by this bunny costume instructable. We scaled the dimensions using Shaked clothes and used green fleece fabric.

The head was cut out of foam and glued using contact cement. The half dome was cut using this pattern \ tutorial. The top head and front beak were drawn and cut freehandedly ( it took a lot of tweaking). We glued three straws to create the illusion of bones. Then we cut some pieces of fabric and sewn them together. The horns were made out of white felt. We used hot glue to hold everything together. The eyes were made from stereophone spheres which we cut in half and drew pupils using a sharpie.

Sewing the concealed zipper was not as hard as I thought, I used this youtube tutorial. (I had to unsew the back for it ‚Äì should have planned ahead).

The purple circles and the stripes of the back were sewn like an applique using a zigzag stitch (far from perfect).¬†
