<p>In this paper, they take on the task of surface reconstruction with a probabilistic twist. They generalize the well-known Poisson Surface reconstruction algorithm and give it a full statistical formalism. Essentially their method quantifies the uncertainty of surface reconstruction from a point cloud. Instead of outputting an implicit function, they represent the shape as a modified Gaussian process. This unique perspective and interpretation enables conducting statistical queries, for example, given a point, is it on the surface? is it inside the shape?</p>
