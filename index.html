Hi, I'm Itzik!

Researcher • Educator • Engineer

About Me

I'm a Research Scientist at Roblox, where I work on avatars and animation. My broader research focuses on 3D computer vision, point cloud processing, and deep learning for 3D understanding. I believe in making research accessible—publishing open-source code and hosting conversations with researchers. PhD from the Technion (plus B.Sc Cum Laude, M.Sc Summa Cum Laude).

Wondering how to say my name? I got you covered.

In my spare time, I host the Talking Papers Podcast, providing a platform for early career academics to discuss their latest research. Here you can explore my publications, code, and blog. Feel free to contact me with any questions!
