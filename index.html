About Me

I am a researcher focused on 3D computer vision, machine learning, and deep learning for 3D point clouds. My work spans surface reconstruction, classification, detection, and action recognition. I received my PhD in 2019 from the Technion – Israel Institute of Technology , where I also earned my B.Sc (Cum Laude 2008) and M.Sc (Summa Cum Laude 2015).

I host the Talking Papers Podcast , providing a platform for early career academics to discuss their latest research. Here you can explore my publications , code , and blog . Feel free to contact me with any questions!
